Baseball Basics:

This site teaches the basics of baseball, including how the game is played and the different positions on the field.

How the game of Baseball is played

Baseball is a game played between two teams of nine players each. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team by hitting a ball and running around four bases in a counterclockwise direction.

The game is divided into nine innings, with each team getting a turn at bat in each inning. Players on the field are divided into offense and defense, with the defensive team trying to get the offensive team out by striking them out, tagging them, or forcing them out. Once the defensive team gets three outs, then the teams switch roles and the other team gets a turn at bat.

Positions in Baseball

There are nine positions in baseball, each with its own unique responsibilities. The positions are:

  • Pitcher
  • Catcher
  • First Base
  • Second Base
  • Third Base
  • Shortstop
  • Left Field
  • Center Field
  • Right Field

How to score a run in Baseball:

  1. The batter hits the ball and runs to first base.
  2. The batter advances to second base.
  3. The batter advances to third base.
  4. The batter then must cross homeplate for the run to count towards their team.
