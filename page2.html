Baseball Stats Explained:

This page explains the most common baseball statistics and what they mean.

Common Baseball Statistics

Baseball statistics are numerical measures that help evaluate a player's performance.

Baseball statistics can be categorized into offensive, defensive, and pitching stats. These stats are important for analyzing player performance and making strategic decisions.

Advanced Baseball Statistics

These stats are just the simpler ones. More advanced stats include WAR (Wins Above Replacement), OPS, and wRC+.

Understanding advanced baseball statistics enhances appreciation of the game and allows fans and analysts to compare players more accurately.
